Dental Care

Steps to Maintain Healthy Teeth & Gums

1Step

Brush Twice a Day

  • Use fluoride toothpaste for better results
  • Tip: Brush for at least 2 minutes

2Step

Floss Daily

  • Removes plaque between teeth
  • Tip: Use gentle motions to avoid gum damage

3Step

Limit Sugary Foods

  • Prevents cavities and enamel erosion
  • Tip: Drink water after eating sweets

4Step

Visit Dentist Regularly

  • Early detection of dental issues
  • Tip: Schedule check-ups every 6 months
